Welcome to the robot combat archive! A site with the goal of centralising information about big-box robot combat globally with a ranking system thrown in for fun!

Did you know that Chaos 2 has beaten Razer, or that Vlad the Impaler fought in Japan? Well with all the information in one place It's much easier to view a robot's whole history and find trivia like this. The archive is currently complete up to 2003.

What this site is:
An overview of a robot's fight history and design changes over time.
What this site is not:
An in-depth break-down of every detail of every robot and fight. For more detail you can often check the excellent Robot Wars or BattleBots wikis.
A definitive answer to the greatest robot of all time or who would beat who. The ranking system has flaws and as an example modern robots are always likely to beat older ones.
